Facts about Dogs

  1. A dog's nose print is as unique as a human fingerprint, containing unique ridges and textures that can be used for identification.
  2. Dogs can detect emotions and even some diseases like cancer by scent, highlighting their extraordinary sense of smell.
  3. Unlike humans, dogs primarily cool themselves by panting and sweating through their paw pads, leaving behind wet footprints.
